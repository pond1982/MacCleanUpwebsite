Safe by design

Items move to the Trash — never instantly deleted — so you can always restore them. System files, your Photos, Mail, Messages, iCloud and live data are protected and never touched. You review every item before anything is cleaned. And because MacCleanUp is sandboxed and runs entirely on your Mac, it collects no data and nothing ever leaves your device.
