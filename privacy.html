Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Mochi Clean Up collects no data. It runs entirely on your Mac. We don't have an account system, we don't use analytics or tracking, and the app makes no network connections. Nothing you scan or clean ever leaves your device, and we never see it.

What the app accesses

  • Folders you grant. Mochi Clean Up is sandboxed and can only look at folders you explicitly grant it through the standard macOS open panel. Your choice is stored on your Mac as a security-scoped bookmark so you don't have to grant it again. You can revoke access at any time in Settings.
  • File information. To find clutter, the app reads file sizes and basic metadata, and your disk's free/used space. This is read on-device to show you results — it is never collected or transmitted.

What the app does with files

When you choose to clean up, selected items are moved to the Trash — never permanently deleted by the app — so you can restore them yourself. System locations, your Photos, Mail and Messages, iCloud, and live data are protected and never touched.

Data collection & sharing

None. We collect no personal data, usage data, or diagnostics. We share nothing with third parties because there is nothing to share. This corresponds to “Data Not Collected” on the app's App Store privacy label.

Children

Mochi Clean Up is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, we'll post the updated version here with a new date.
